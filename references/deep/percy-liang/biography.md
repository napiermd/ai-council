## Career

### Stanford faculty (2012 – present)
- Joined Stanford CS as assistant professor
- Full professor with courtesy appointment in Statistics
- **Senior Fellow, Stanford HAI**

### Teaching
- **CS336: Language Models from Scratch** — his canonical Stanford
  graduate course, building a modern LM pipeline from scratch
- Also teaches foundation-model and NLP seminars

### CRFM — founding director (August 18, 2021 – present)
- **Center for Research on Foundation Models** — an initiative
  within **Stanford HAI** (not independent)
- **175+ researchers across 10+ Stanford departments** (medicine,
  law, political science, philosophy, economics, CS)
- **Four research pillars:**
  1. Technical foundations
  2. Beneficial applications
  3. Societal impact
  4. AI policy
- Mission (verbatim): *"aims to make fundamental advances in the
  study, development, and deployment of foundation models"*
- Positioning (verbatim): *"Foundation models present novel
  capabilities and risks that are poorly understood. Nonetheless,
  they are being deployed at scale."*
- **Associated faculty** (non-exhaustive): Rishi Bommasani (society
  lead, FMTI lead), Christopher Manning, Christopher Ré,
  Tatsunori Hashimoto, Dan Jurafsky, Surya Ganguli, Peter
  Henderson
