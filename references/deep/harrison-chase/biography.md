# Biography: Harrison Chase

> Co-founder and CEO of LangChain. Author of LangGraph and
> LangSmith. Most-invited podcast guest on AI agents in 2024–2026.
> Not an academic author — a practitioner-thought-leader whose
> canon is blog posts and OSS commits.

## Career

### Pre-LangChain
- **Kensho Technologies** — early career (per LinkedIn secondary
  references; not independently verified from primary sources)
- **Robust Intelligence** — led the ML team. This is where he met
  Ankush Gola (co-founder). Robust Intelligence is an MLOps / ML
  testing startup. Chase's focus was applied ML engineering

### LangChain founding
- **October 16, 2022** — first commits to the `langchain` repo
  from Chase's personal GitHub (`hwchase17`). Nights/weekends side
  project while at Robust Intelligence
- **October 25, 2022** — `langchain` 0.0.1 live (nine days after
  first commit)
- **Late 2022 / early 2023** — left Robust Intelligence full-time
- **February 2023** — incorporated LangChain, Inc. with Ankush
  Gola
- Chase quote on the transition (Fortune, Oct 2025):
  > *"It was very crazy. I didn't know I was going to leave my
  > previous job. I had no clue what I was going to do next."*

### LangChain, Inc. (Feb 2023 – present)
- Co-founder and CEO
- **NOT a YC company** — common misattribution. Venture-funded
  directly (Benchmark seed)
