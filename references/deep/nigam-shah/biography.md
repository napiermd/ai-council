# Biography: Nigam Shah

## Name and credentials

Nigam H. Shah, MBBS, PhD.

## Training

- **MBBS** — trained as a physician in India.
- **PhD** — biomedical informatics (computational / ontology-era
  training that grounds his later work in observational health data).
- Moved to the US and joined Stanford; in his own framing, "everybody
  told me that physicians who get into AI stuff all go to Stanford, so
  that's where I went." (Define Ventures, 2025.)

His career narrative: *"a series of fortunate accidents."* (Define
Ventures, 2025.)

  Stanford ISP Star Award (2025).

## Career arc (synthesis)

**Ontology / informatics era (pre-2015):** OBO Foundry co-author,
observational health data standards, OMOP/OHDSI foundational work.
**PLoS ONE 2015** proton-pump-inhibitor/MI study is the prototype of
his later Green Button work — mining EHR data to answer real clinical
questions at scale.

**Green Button era (2014–2019):** Health Affairs 2014 "A Green Button
for Using Aggregate Patient Data at the Point of Care" plants the
thesis. npj Digital Medicine 2019 "It is time to learn from patients
like mine" refines it. NEJM Catalyst 2021 "Using Aggregate Patient
Data at the Bedside via an On-Demand Consultation Service" operationalizes
it as a production service.

**Delivery science era (2020–present):** npj DM 2020 "Developing a
delivery science for AI" reframes the field. MINIMAR (JAMIA 2020),
Model Facts Labels (npj DM 2020), and subsequent framework papers
build out the operational layer.

**Policy / infrastructure era (2022–present):** Chief Data Scientist
appointment at Stanford Health Care (March 2022), CHAI co-founding
(late 2022), STAT op-ed with Halamka and Saria (March 2022 "Health-
related AI needs rigorous evaluation and guardrails"). JAMA 2024
assurance-labs piece. FURM paper (NEJM Catalyst 2024). NAM Vital
Directions contribution (Health Affairs 2025).

**LLM era (2023–present):** JAMA 2023 "Creation and Adoption of LLMs
in Medicine" (with Entwistle and Pfeffer). MedAlign (2023). EHRSHOT
(NeurIPS 2023 spotlight). Systematic review (JAMA 2025). MedHELM
(Nature Medicine 2026). Clinical Environment Simulator (CES, 2025).
