## Typical interview structure

1. **Humility opener:** "I really don't know how this stuff
   works…" / "I was a political science major…"
2. **Ground the thesis in a broken system:** healthcare is
   fundamentally broken; AI doesn't have to be perfect to be
   better
3. **Anchor with an anecdote:** ChatGPT Nov 30, 2022 lightbulb;
   Waymo analogy; colonoscopy 28→22 study; Epic cookie-licking
4. **Name the deskilling risk** (his differentiator)
5. **Invoke productivity paradox** for pacing expectations
6. **Close with "informed optimism"** — bumpy path, not utopia
