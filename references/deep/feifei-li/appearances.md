## Typical talk structure

1. **Deep-time opener** (Cambrian; Alan Turing; evolutionary
   sight)
2. **Science history anchor** (Bayesian vision, WordNet,
   ImageNet)
3. **Personal pivot** (mother, father, Parsippany dry cleaner)
4. **Civilizational-scale framing** (AI as civilizational
   technology)
5. **Refusal of extremes** (not utopian, not doomer)
6. **Call to shared action** (human-centered AI, with/for/by)
