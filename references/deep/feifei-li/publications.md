## Top-cited canonical papers

### 1. ImageNet — "A large-scale hierarchical image database" (CVPR 2009)

**Authors:** Jia Deng, Wei Dong, Richard Socher, Li-Jia Li, Kai
Li, **Fei-Fei Li** (senior author / project PI).

- **Citations:** **94,058** (#1 on her Scholar profile, April
  2026)
- **Role:** Senior / last author; Jia Deng was her PhD student
  leading the data construction
- **Initial venue:** CVPR 2009, Miami — initially a **poster**,
  not an oral

### 2. ILSVRC — "ImageNet Large Scale Visual Recognition Challenge" (IJCV 2015)

**Authors:** Olga Russakovsky, Jia Deng, Hao Su, Jonathan Krause,
Sanjeev Satheesh, Sean Ma, Zhiheng Huang, Andrej Karpathy, Aditya
Khosla, Michael Bernstein, Alexander C. Berg, **Fei-Fei Li**
(senior author).

- **Citations:** **54,789** (#2 on her Scholar profile)
- **arXiv:** 1409.0575
- **Significance:** Canonical description of ILSVRC 2010-2014,
  including the 2012 AlexNet moment that triggered the modern
  deep-learning era

### 3. Perceptual Losses — "for Real-Time Style Transfer and Super-Resolution" (ECCV 2016)

**Authors:** Justin Johnson, Alexandre Alahi, **Fei-Fei Li**
(senior author).

- **Citations:** **14,630**
- Justin Johnson is a World Labs co-founder

### 4. "Large-scale video classification with convolutional neural networks" (CVPR 2014)

**Authors:** Andrej Karpathy et al., **Fei-Fei Li** (senior
author).

- **Citations:** **9,330**

### 5. Foundation Models — "On the Opportunities and Risks of Foundation Models" (2021)

**Authors:** Rishi Bommasani et al. (114+ authors) — **Fei-Fei
Li** among co-authors.

- **Citations:** **9,346**
- Written at Stanford CRFM (sits within HAI)
- Note: Percy Liang is senior author; Li is a co-author

### 6. Visual Genome — "Connecting Language and Vision Using Crowdsourced Dense Image Annotations" (IJCV 2017)

**Authors:** Ranjay Krishna, Yuke Zhu, Oliver Groth, Justin
Johnson, Kenji Hata, Joshua Kravitz, Stephanie Chen, Yannis
Kalantidis, Li-Jia Li, David A. Shamma, Michael S. Bernstein,
**Fei-Fei Li**.

- **Significance:** Dense region-level annotations (objects,
  attributes, relationships) — foundational for VQA and
  scene-graph research

## World Labs blog posts (authored or co-signed)

### "3D as code" — March 3, 2026
> *"3D is becoming the universal interface for space. It's the
> medium that allows humans and AI systems to generate, edit,
> simulate, and share worlds together."*

Predicts a hybrid stack: structured 3D worlds (code) → neural
graphics (language) → simulation engines (chips). Rejects pure
end-to-end generative video as the endpoint for world models.

### "Streaming 3DGS worlds on the web" / Spark 2.0 — April 14, 2026 (in-window)
- Streamable Level-of-Detail 3DGS
- Continuous LoD + progressive streaming + GPU virtual memory
- Renders **>100 million splats on mobile** via Three.js + WebGL2
- **Open-sourced**

### "Announcing the World API" — January 21, 2026
Public API for generating explorable 3D worlds from text / image
/ video inputs.
