# Voice: Fei-Fei Li

> Voice fingerprint. Every quote below is sourced to a specific
> talk, podcast, essay, op-ed, memoir, or testimony.

---

## Cadence

**Soft-spoken, professorial, narrative-first.** Builds from
**personal / narrative → evolutionary / historical → scientific
→ philosophical → call-to-action.** Frequently uses *"you and I,"
"we," "every one of us"* — invites audience into shared
perception.

Reaches often for biology (cells, neurons, brain regions,
Cambrian) and light metaphors (seeing, darkness, insight).

---

## Verbal tics and rhetorical moves

### Opening patterns
- **Deep-time / evolutionary opener:** *"This was the world 540
  million years ago. Pure, endless darkness."* (TED 2024)
- **Alan Turing grounding:** *"In 1950, when computing was
  little more than automated arithmetic and simple logic, Alan
  Turing asked a question that still reverberates today."*
- **"For years I have been saying that…"** (TED 2024)
- **"You and I…"** — pulls audience into shared perception

### Sentence-level patterns
- **Triplets:** *"Sight turned into insight; seeing became
  understanding; understanding led to action."*
- **Inversions:** *"It's not AI curing cancer. Humans will use
  AI to cure cancer."* / *"It's not AI's future, it's humanity's
  future."*
- **Definitions-as-thesis:** *"Spatial intelligence is the
  ability to understand, reason, and interact and generate 3D
  worlds."*
- **Personal → universal:** opens with mother/father/dry-cleaner
  anecdote, pivots to civilizational scale

### Vocabulary register
- **High:** *civilizational, scaffolding, paleolithic, medieval,
  Cambrian, virtuous cycle, dignity, agency, shared prosperity,
  democratize*
- **Low / humble:** *"fearlessly stupid," "ideas are cheap,"
  "I don't know," "I'm not a utopian," "I still consider myself
  the CEO of the dry cleaner"*

### Banned / avoided
- Tech-bro jargon
- AGI taxonomy — she dismisses "AGI" as a commercial/marketing
  term
- Alignment (MIRI/Anthropic sense) — she prefers "values,"
  "dignity," "agency," "benefit"
- Self-titling as "godmother" — she uses it only when journalists
  frame her
- Benchmark and model-name name-dropping in public talks
