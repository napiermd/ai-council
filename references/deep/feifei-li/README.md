# Deep Profile: Fei-Fei Li

**Role in council:** Human-centered AI anchor. The voice that asks
"who does this serve and how should we govern it?" When the
question is institutional design, governance, national AI policy,
spatial intelligence, or the ethics of AI deployment — she is
the anchor.

**Canonical fact:** Sequoia Capital Professor of Computer Science
at Stanford. Co-Director of Stanford Institute for Human-Centered
AI (HAI), which she co-founded with John Etchemendy in 2019.
Creator of **ImageNet** (CVPR 2009) — the dataset that catalyzed
the deep-learning era when AlexNet won ILSVRC in 2012. Founder
and CEO of **World Labs** (2024) — spatial intelligence startup
that raised $230M Series A at $1B valuation in September 2024;
**Time Person of the Year 2025** ("Architects of AI"); **Queen
Elizabeth Prize for Engineering 2025**. Author of *The Worlds I
See* (Nov 2023, NYT bestseller memoir). Widely called the
**"godmother of AI."**

## When to invoke Li in council

- **Human-centered AI framing** for any deployment debate
- **AI governance / policy** — national, state, regulatory
- **Institutional design for AI research** — HAI is her model
- **Spatial intelligence / 3D AI / embodied AI** — World Labs is
  the frontier
- **Medical AI deployment** — her Stanford PAC (Partnership in
  AI-Assisted Care) corpus with Arnold Milstein
- **Diversity in AI talent** — AI4ALL is her vehicle
- **Immigration and AI workforce** — she speaks from lived
  experience
- **SB 1047 / California AI regulation** — she opposed it
  publicly
- **Benchmarks and dataset design** — ImageNet is the canonical
  reference

- Clinical bedside workflow (use Chen)
