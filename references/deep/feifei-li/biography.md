# Biography: Fei-Fei Li

> Born July 3, 1976, Beijing; grew up in Chengdu. Sequoia Capital
> Professor of CS at Stanford. Denning Family Co-Director of
> Stanford HAI (co-founded 2019 with John Etchemendy). Founder
> and CEO of World Labs (2024). Creator of ImageNet (CVPR 2009).
> Author of *The Worlds I See* (Nov 2023).

---

## Training path

### Childhood / immigration
- Born **July 3, 1976, Beijing**; raised in **Chengdu, Sichuan**
- Father emigrated to **Parsippany, NJ** when she was 12
- She and her mother joined him at **age 16 (1992)**
- Parents operated a dry-cleaning shop; she worked weekends there
  through high school and college
- **Parsippany High School**, graduated **1995**

### Undergraduate
- **Princeton University** — B.A. Physics **1999**

### Graduate
- **Caltech** — M.S. Electrical Engineering **2001**
- **Caltech** — Ph.D. Electrical Engineering **2005**
- **Dissertation:** *"Visual Recognition: Computational Models
  and Human Psychophysics"*
- **Advisor:** **Pietro Perona** (primary)
- **Co-advisor:** **Christof Koch**

### Early career (academic)
- **University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign** — Assistant
  Professor, ECE, 2005-2006
- **Princeton CS** — Assistant Professor 2007-2009
- **Stanford** — Assistant Professor (2009) → Associate with
  tenure (2012) → **Full Professor (2018)**

## Career at Stanford

### SAIL Director — 2013-2018
Director of the Stanford Artificial Intelligence Laboratory.

### Google Cloud — Jan 2017 to Fall 2018 (sabbatical)
**Vice President and Chief Scientist of AI/ML, Google Cloud.**

### Stanford HAI — co-founding co-director, March 2019
- Named co-director October 2018; public launch March 2019
- **Co-director:** **John Etchemendy** — Patrick Suppes Family
  Professor in the School of Humanities and Sciences; former
  Stanford Provost (2000-2017); philosophy of logic
- **Her title within HAI:** **Denning Family Co-Director**
- **Mission (verbatim):** *"to advance AI research, education,
  policy and practice to improve the human condition."*
- **Founding statement:** *"the next frontier in AI cannot
  simply be technological — it must be humanistic as well."*
- **CRFM sits within HAI** — Percy Liang directs; the 2021
  "Foundation Models" paper is a CRFM output with Li as a
  listed co-author

## World Labs — founder & CEO, January 2024

### Co-founders (all 4)
- **Fei-Fei Li** — Co-founder & CEO
- **Justin Johnson** — Co-founder; her former PhD student;
  University of Michigan CS faculty; co-author on Visual Genome
  and Perceptual Losses
- **Christoph Lassner** — Co-founder; ex-Meta Reality Labs;
  pioneer of neural rendering / Gaussian splatting
- **Ben Mildenhall** — Co-founder; NeRF co-creator; ex-Google
  Research

### Funding
- **Early seed 2024:** reported seed round; valuation passed $1B
  by July 2024 per Bloomberg/TechCrunch
- **Series A — September 13, 2024:** **$230M total** at **$1B+
  valuation**
  - **Co-leads:** Andreessen Horowitz (a16z), NEA, Radical
    Ventures
  - **Participants:** NVIDIA NVentures, AMD Ventures, Intel
    Capital, Databricks Ventures, Adobe Ventures
  - **Angels:** Marc Benioff, Ashton Kutcher
- **Series B — February 2026:** reported ~$1.4B+ raised, ~$5B
  valuation (trade-press verified; see recent-30d.md)

### Mission (verbatim)
> *"Building the next frontier of generative AI — one where
> models can understand and interact with the world to empower
> use cases from storytelling to simulation."*

### Thesis — "Spatial intelligence"
AI that perceives, reasons about, generates, and interacts with
3D worlds. Framing: *"3D as code"* — space as the universal
interface.

### Products (2026)
- **Marble** — flagship spatial/world generator producing
  *"spatially consistent, high-fidelity, and persistent 3D
  worlds"* from text, images, videos, or 360° panoramas
- **Spark** — JavaScript framework for streaming 3D Gaussian
  Splatting worlds in the browser; Spark 2.0 deep dive April
  14, 2026

## AI4ALL — co-founded 2017 (from SAILORS precursor 2015)

- **2015:** precursor **SAILORS** (Stanford AI Lab OutReach
  Summers) founded
- **2017:** renamed "AI4ALL @ Stanford" and spun out as the
  nonprofit **AI4ALL**
- **Verified co-founders:** **Fei-Fei Li**, **Olga Russakovsky**
  (her former PhD student; now Princeton faculty), **Rick Sommer**
- **Early backers / sponsors:** **Melinda French Gates**,
  **Jensen Huang** (both often incorrectly listed as co-founders)
- **Note:** Jim Fan (NVIDIA, embodied AI) was Fei-Fei's PhD
  student — **NOT** an AI4ALL co-founder. Common misattribution.
