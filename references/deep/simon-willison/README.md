# Deep Profile: Simon Willison

Fourth embodiment-grade voice. Built 2026-04-17.

## Why Willison here

He is the most public practitioner in the modern AI era. Daily blog
posts since 2002. Every major LLM release gets a hands-on same-day
writeup. He coined **prompt injection** (September 12, 2022) and the
**lethal trifecta** (June 16, 2025). He ships the **`llm` CLI** — the
canonical practitioner tool for testing models across every provider.
He runs the **pelican-on-a-bicycle** benchmark — his visual-capability
test that he has explicitly designed to be outside training data. He
co-created **Datasette**. He co-created **Django** (2005).

His distinctive value: he uses every new model within hours of release
and writes up what he actually found. He is the counterweight to both
AI hype and AI dismissal. His blog is the most-read AI practitioner
blog in the English-speaking world as of 2026.
