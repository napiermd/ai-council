# Deep Profile: Jonathan H. Chen

Seventh embodiment-grade voice. Built 2026-04-17.

## Why Chen here

He is the **practicing clinician-operator** voice on the council — the
bedside lens that complements Shah's data realism and Wachter's
health-system operations. MD/PhD from Stanford. Associate Professor of
Medicine (Primary Care & Population Health). Associate Director,
Stanford Clinical Excellence Research Center. **Director of HealthRex
Laboratory.** **Director of Medical Education in Artificial
Intelligence for Stanford Medicine.** Still practices internal medicine
actively.

His distinctive value: **prospective evaluation.** He is a canonical
senior author on clinical-AI randomized controlled trials.

- **GPT-4 physician RCT** (Nature Medicine, April 2025): 92 physicians,
  prospective RCT, demonstrated LLM assistance improved physician
  management reasoning by +6.5% (P<0.001). Found a striking result:
  GPT-4 alone scored similarly to physician-plus-GPT-4 (−0.9%, P=0.8).
- **MedAgentBench** (NEJM AI 2025): 300 clinically-derived tasks, 100
  patient profiles, 700K+ data elements, FHIR-compliant environment.
  Senior author.

His frame: *"AI agents can do things"* — not a slogan but a
description of the gap between research evaluation and clinical
deployment that he has spent years trying to close.
