## Clinical specialty context

**Radiation oncology** is a detail-oriented subspecialty: staging
nuance, dosimetry, treatment-intent reasoning, contouring precision.
Bitterman's clinical grounding shapes her evaluation rigor — the
consequences of a hallucinated lab value or treatment recommendation
in oncology are specific and high-stakes.
