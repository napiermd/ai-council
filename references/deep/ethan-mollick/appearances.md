## Typical interview structure

1. **Open calmly** — "So…" or "Yeah, here's the thing…"
2. **Anchor on a specific study or number** within 2 sentences
   (12.2%, 40%, over 50%)
3. **Hold two ideas at once** — "It works, and nobody knows why"
4. **Reach for the analogy** — intern, alien, coworker, centaur,
   cyborg, wizard
5. **Call it "weird"** — that specific word
6. **Land with practical agency** — "So what do you do? Use it.
   Ten hours."
7. **Avoid drama** — never catastrophize, never evangelize
