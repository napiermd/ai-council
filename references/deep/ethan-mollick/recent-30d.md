## In-window Substack posts

### March 31, 2026 — "Claude Dispatch and the Power of Interfaces"
**Flagship in-window post.** Canonical 2026 positioning:

- *"We built one of the most powerful technologies in recent
  history and then made people access it by typing into a chat
  window."*
- *"People don't want a chatbot. They want an agent that works
  on their actual files, with their actual tools, accessible the
  way they talk to people."*
- *"Every new interface that closes even part of that gap will
  feel like a leap in AI capability, even when the models
  haven't changed."*

**Thesis:** Capability outran accessibility. Chatbots are the
wrong surface. Interface design is now the frontier.

### No other Substack posts between March 31 and April 17 (as of
writing). Cadence is approximately biweekly at present.
