## Licensing philosophy

- **Aya (via Cohere Labs):** open weights, research use (CC
  non-commercial) — largest open multilingual model family
- **Command A (commercial):** weights on Hugging Face under
  **CC-BY-NC** (not commercial open source)
- **Stance:** open for research, commercial under license

This is a hybrid open / closed stance — different from Meta
(fully open-weight), Anthropic (fully closed), and Mistral
(mixed commercial open).
