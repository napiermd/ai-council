## Typical interview structure

1. **Open calmly** with the prosaic / enterprise framing
2. **Anchor on margins, deployment model, or customer story**
3. **Pivot to contrast-as-definition** — "that's the difference
   between X and Y"
4. **Hedged affirmative** — "it's definitely true… it's also the
   dumbest"
5. **Operator verbs** — deploy, flip, ship, capture
6. **Short declarative closer** — *"So I can't wait."*
