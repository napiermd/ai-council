# Biography: Aidan Gomez

> Born late 1996/1997, Toronto, Ontario; grew up in Brighton, ON.
> Canadian (Wikipedia claims "British-Canadian" — dual citizenship
> unverified). Co-founder and CEO of Cohere since 2019. One of
> 8 authors of "Attention Is All You Need" (NeurIPS 2017).
> Oxford DPhil in CS granted 2024. Rivian board member (April
> 2025).

---

## Training path

### Early life
- **Grew up in Brighton, Ontario** (small-town Canada)
- **Worked with Roger Grosse at U Toronto as an undergrad**

### Undergraduate
- **University of Toronto** — BSc Computer Science and
  Mathematics (Honours), **2018**

### Summer 2017 — Google Brain intern (age 20)
- Co-authored **"Attention Is All You Need"** during this
  internship
- Worked alongside Jakob Uszkoreit, Łukasz Kaiser, Jeff Dean
- Geoffrey Hinton was in the broader Google Brain Toronto org
- **Founded FOR.ai** (June 2017) — distributed collaborative
  research collective. Later rebranded **Cohere Labs in April
  2025**.

### 2018 (9 months)
Further Google Brain work, including with Hinton and Kevin
Swersky on knowledge distillation and multi-task learning.

### PhD
- **University of Oxford** — DPhil (PhD) in Computer Science,
  **2018-2023** (OATML page). **Granted in absentia 2024** per
  Wikipedia.
- **Supervisors:** **Yarin Gal and Yee Whye Teh** (confirmed via
  Oxford OATML page)
- **Lab:** OATML (Oxford Applied Machine Learning Team)
- **Funding:** Clarendon Scholar; Open Philanthropy AI Fellow
- **Answer to the common question:** He did complete his Oxford
  DPhil — granted 2024 after paused studies.
