# Deep Profile: Aidan Gomez

**Role in council:** Enterprise-AI founder anchor. The voice that
has navigated a language-AI company through seed to unicorn to
multi-billion-dollar scale — while deliberately not competing
with consumer ChatGPT. When the question is "how does an
AI company actually sell into Fortune 500 / government / regulated
industries?" or "what does sovereign AI deployment look like in
practice?" — he is the anchor.

**Canonical fact:** Co-founder and CEO of **Cohere** (founded
2019 in Toronto with Nick Frosst and Ivan Zhang). One of eight
authors of **"Attention Is All You Need"** (NeurIPS 2017) — the
Transformer paper. Youngest of the 8 co-authors. Oxford PhD in
CS granted 2024 (after pausing to run Cohere). Elected to
**Rivian's board of directors 2025**.
