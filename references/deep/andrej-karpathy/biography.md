# Biography: Andrej Karpathy

## Current role

CEO and founder, **Eureka Labs** (announced July 16, 2024) — AI-native
education company. First product: **LLM101n**, a course that teaches you
to build a Storyteller AI LLM from scratch in Python, C, and CUDA.

## Career timeline

| Years | Role |
|---|---|
| 2024–present | Founder, Eureka Labs + educator on YouTube (Zero to Hero, Deep Dive into LLMs, How I Use LLMs) |
| 2023–Feb 2024 | Returned to OpenAI; worked on midtraining + synthetic data |
| 2017–2022 | Senior Director of AI, Tesla Autopilot (vision stack, Autopilot AI team) |
| 2015–2017 | Research Scientist + founding member, OpenAI |
| 2011–2015 | PhD, Stanford, under Fei-Fei Li. Designed and taught **CS 231n** (the canonical computer-vision course) |
| 2009–2011 | MSc, University of British Columbia |
| 2005–2009 | BSc, University of Toronto (CS + physics major, math minor) |

## Departures — public framings

## Teaching record

- **Stanford CS 231n** (Convolutional Neural Networks for Visual
  Recognition) — grew under his stewardship from 150 → 750 students.
  Course notes at https://cs231n.github.io/ are among the most-cited
  deep-learning pedagogy on the web.
- **Neural Networks: Zero to Hero** — 8+ YouTube lectures totaling ~15
  hours. Canonical practitioner on-ramp to modern LLMs. Paired with
  `nn-zero-to-hero` and `makemore`/`nanoGPT`/`minbpe`.
- **General-audience track (2023–2025):**
  - "[1hr Talk] Intro to Large Language Models" (Nov 2023)
  - "Deep Dive into LLMs like ChatGPT" (~3h31m, Feb 2025)
  - "How I use LLMs" (Feb 2025)
