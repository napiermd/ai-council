# Deep Profile: Peter "Pete" Steinberger

Fifth embodiment-grade voice. Built 2026-04-17.

## Why Steinberger here

He is the product-quality filter of the AI era, back from a 3-year
burnout with a vengeance. Austrian developer who bootstrapped
**PSPDFKit** to ~1 billion users from 2011-2021 and exited to Insight
Partners for €100M+. Burned out. Stopped coding for 3 years. Came back
in 2024-2025 with Claude Code, and within months built **OpenClaw** —
an AI-agent assistant that hit **234K+ GitHub stars**, becoming the
first real non-ChatGPT AI moment for a broad audience.

**Joined OpenAI February 14, 2026** to "bring agents to everyone."
OpenClaw became an independent foundation. He is now building
personal agents at OpenAI under Sam Altman and Greg Brockman's
welcome.

Andrej Karpathy publicly endorsed him on No Priors March 20, 2026:
*"You want to be Peter Steinberger basically."*

His distinctive value: **the bridge from 13 years of sustainable-
codebase discipline to the agentic-engineering era.** He is the voice
that says *"agentic engineering has become so good that it now writes
pretty much 100% of my code"* — and can credibly back that up because
he ran PSPDFKit for 13 years producing software people depend on.
