## PSPDFKit (2010-2021)

- **Product:** PDF and document-processing SDK for iOS, Android, web.
- **Customers:** Dropbox, Evernote, IBM, SAP — embedded in apps
  reaching ~1 billion devices.
- **Exit:** October 4, 2021 — majority investment from **Insight
  Partners** ($116M round reported). Pete moved to advisory role.
- **Culture he built:** written-proposal-before-implementation,
  documented in his 2019 blog "How We Work at PSPDFKit."
- **Canonical talk:** *"Building a Sustainable Codebase: 7 Years and
  Counting"* — CRAFT Budapest 2017.
  - Thesis: *"you can both 'ship it' and have a sustainable product
    that allows you to move fast and where new people don't run away
    screaming."*

**Correction:** Pete did **not** found Emerge Tools. That company was
founded by Josh Cohenzadeh and Noah Martin (YC W21), acquired by
Sentry May 2025. Pete's 2021-2024 was a hiatus, not a second company.

### What it is
- Cross-platform personal AI assistant ("any OS, any platform")
- Written in TypeScript + Swift
- Local-first gateway controlling sessions, channels, tools, events
- Multi-channel inbox: WhatsApp, Telegram, Slack, Discord, iMessage,
  etc.

  (`SKILL.md` files)
- Mascot: **Molty**, a space lobster (origin of the 🦞)
- MIT license. Foundation-governed.
- Sponsors: OpenAI, GitHub, NVIDIA, Vercel, Blacksmith, Convex

## Joining OpenAI (February 2026)

- **Announcement:** February 14, 2026 ("OpenClaw, OpenAI and the
  future" on steipete.me + X post).
- **Start date:** February 16, 2026.
- **Role:** bring agents to everyone; next-generation personal agents
  under Sam Altman.
- **Compensation (undisclosed):** reportedly "well under $1B" but both
  OpenAI and Meta valued the deal in the billions.

### The bidding war
- **Competing bidders:** OpenAI, Meta (Zuckerberg personally WhatsApp-
  called Pete), xAI
- Zuckerberg tested OpenClaw locally and gave direct feedback; Pete
  called the personal engagement "the biggest compliment."
- Pete chose OpenAI because of **stronger open-source guarantees** on
  keeping OpenClaw independent.
- **Arrangement:** mirrors Chrome/Chromium — open foundation + OpenAI-
  supported commercial version.
