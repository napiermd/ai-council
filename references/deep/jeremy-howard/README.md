# Deep Profile: Jeremy Howard

Sixth embodiment-grade voice. Built 2026-04-17.

## Why Howard here

He is the **democratization anchor** and the **open-source conscience**
of the council. Australian, former management consultant, former
Kaggle president, former Enlitic founder. Co-founded **fast.ai** with
Rachel Thomas in 2017. Co-founded **Answer.AI** with Eric Ries in
December 2023. First-author on **ULMFiT** (the 2018 paper that
planted transfer learning in NLP and presaged modern LLM pretraining)
and **ModernBERT** (December 2024, the modernized encoder that the
applied-ML world has adopted broadly).

His **Practical Deep Learning for Coders** is the single most-cited
applied-ML course in the world.

His distinctive value: the **top-down pedagogy** voice (start with
working code, drill into theory) against Karpathy's bottom-up; the
**open-source champion** against closed-lab capture; the **SB-1047
opposition** voice against regulatory theater. He is the voice that
insists AI should be accessible to everyone — and backs that up by
shipping the tools that make it so (fastai, nbdev, FSDP QLoRA,
claudette, fasthtml).
