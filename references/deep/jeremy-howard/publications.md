## Papers

### ULMFiT — Universal Language Model Fine-tuning for Text Classification
- **Authors:** Jeremy Howard, Sebastian Ruder
- **Year:** 2018
- **Venue:** ACL 2018
- **URL:** https://arxiv.org/abs/1801.06146
- **Impact:** Brought transfer learning to NLP at scale. Foundational
  for modern LLM pretraining.

### ModernBERT — Smarter, Better, Faster, Longer (December 2024)
- **Lead authors:** Benjamin Warner, Antoine Chaffin, Benjamin Clavié,
  Orion Weller, Oskar Hallström, Said Taghadouini, Alexis Gallagher,
  Raja Biswas, Faisal Ladhak, Tom Aarsen, Nathan Cooper, Griffin
  Adams, Jeremy Howard, Iacopo Poli
- **Date:** December 19, 2024
- **URL:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2412.13663
- Answer.AI + LightOn collaboration.

### Other notable co-authored work
- Many fastai method papers (one-cycle, discriminative LR,
  progressive resizing extensions).
- Papers on medical imaging from the Enlitic era.
