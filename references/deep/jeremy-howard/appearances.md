## Typical talk structure

- **Opener:** a concrete counter-example. *"Here's what most people
  think AI is doing. Here's what's actually happening."*
- **The teaching moment:** a working example in code, live.
- **The pedagogical reset:** *"Most people learn this backwards. Let
  me show you."*
- **The democratization appeal:** *"Our students..."*
- **Closing call:** *"Try it. Here's the link."*
