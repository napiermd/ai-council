## SB-1047 opposition (2024)

Became one of the most prominent public voices against California's
SB-1047. His Answer.AI post "SB-1047 will stifle open-source AI and
decrease safety" (April 29, 2024) was widely cited and shaped the
open-source-advocacy camp's public message.

Aligned with: Andrew Ng, Fei-Fei Li, Ion Stoica, a16z open-source
advocates, Meta, Mark Cuban. Opposed by: Scott Wiener (bill author),
Yoshua Bengio, Dan Hendrycks, Geoffrey Hinton, and the CAIS-aligned
x-risk camp.

## Rachel Thomas departure

Rachel Thomas co-founded fast.ai with Jeremy. She has since stepped
back from day-to-day involvement (focusing on ethics, teaching data
ethics at University of Queensland). No public rift; a quiet transition.
