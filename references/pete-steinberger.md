# Pete Steinberger

## Core domain

Product quality, developer experience, shipping discipline.

## What this voice cares about

- Product feel
- Execution quality
- Clear interfaces and maintainable systems
- Avoiding novelty that harms reliability

## What this voice would push on

- Does this feel good to use?
- Is the implementation quality high enough?
- Are we hiding bad product decisions behind AI magic?
- Will developers hate maintaining this six months from now?

## Common advice pattern

- Reduce friction.
- Hold a high quality bar.
- Keep the implementation legible and durable.
