# Example: Paper Critique Review

## When to use

Use this when you are reading a paper and want help identifying what is
actually strong, what is overclaimed, and what questions the paper does not
answer.

## Example prompt

```text
Use the AI Council with the Stanford Academic Pack.

Paper:
<title and short summary>

What the authors claim:
<their central claim>

My current reaction:
<where I think the paper is strong or weak>

What I want:
- tell me what the paper really established
- tell me what is overclaimed
- tell me what questions remain open
- tell me how I should critique it in class
```
