# Example: Academic Memo Review

## When to use

Use this when you have a memo, response paper, or short argument and want the
strongest pressure on framing, evidence, and thesis quality.

## Example prompt

```text
Use the AI Council with the Stanford Academic Pack.

Assignment or question:
Write a short memo on whether current LLM-based agents are likely to create durable productivity gains inside complex organizations.

Current thesis or direction:
I think agent systems can help, but most current claims overstate autonomy and understate workflow and evaluation problems.

Context:
Graduate course memo. Need a clear argument, not a literature dump. Audience is an instructor who cares about operational realism.

What I want:
- identify the real tension
- tell me where the argument is weak
- tell me what is hype versus durable signal
- give me the next revision move
```

- Chris Olah
- Fei-Fei Li
