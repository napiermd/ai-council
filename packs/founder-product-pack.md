# Founder Product Pack

Use this pack when you are deciding what AI product to build, how narrowly to
scope it, or whether the current direction is sharp enough to ship.

## Default voices

- Pete Steinberger
- Chip Huyen
- Jeremy Howard

Add Karpathy only if model behavior or architecture is central to the decision.

## Best for

- founder tools
- AI product wedges
- developer products
- deciding between broad platform versus narrow workflow
- pressure-testing whether a product is real or overbuilt

## What to provide

- the decision in front of you
- the user and workflow
- what you are currently leaning toward
- constraints on time, team, and scope
- what success looks like

## What the answer must include

- the real product tension
- whether the current scope is too broad or too narrow
- what to cut
- what to test first
- the next shipping move

## Common failure modes this pack should catch

- category story instead of product
- broad AI surface with no indispensable workflow
- memory or agents used as decoration rather than value
- architecture decisions made before product sharpness

## Copyable prompt

```text
Use the AI Council with the Founder Product Pack.

I want Pete Steinberger, Chip Huyen, and Jeremy Howard active by default.
Add another voice only if it is truly needed.

Decision:
<what I am deciding>

Current direction:
<what I am leaning toward>

User and workflow:
<who this is for and what they are trying to do>

Constraints:
<time, team, budget, stack, product constraints>

What I want:
- name the real product tension
- tell me what to cut
- tell me what to test first
- give me the next move
```
